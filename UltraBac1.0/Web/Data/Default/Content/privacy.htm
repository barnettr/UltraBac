UltraBac Software: Protecting Your Privacy

UltraBac Software is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy discloses our practice of utilizing and sharing personally identifiable information. Please read the complete privacy policy and, at the conclusion, we will direct you on how to contact us if you have any questions.

This site does contain links to other Web sites. If you use a link to visit the site of another company, UltraBac Software is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of that company or its site.

Collecting Information

Collection of email addresses from individuals and organizations interested in our software is a standard business practice.

All information we collect is provided directly by you, with your complete knowledge. Browsing on our Web site will not leave us with any of your personally identifiable information, but, in some cases, will result in the assignment of a cookie, if you have your browser set to accept cookies. We use cookies to put information into forms for you so that you will not have to re-enter information every time you submit information to us. The cookie is stored on your browser, not on our server.

Participation in specific activities on our Web site, such as a contest, mailing list, download, email, or transaction, is voluntary and may require you to share personally identifiable information. We may also ask for your opinions through an online survey, but this survey information will be used for research purposes only to help us build a better Web site and product for you, our customer.

Information Usage

UltraBac Software uses some of your your information for the purpose of allowing you full accessibility to our online services. The data requested is necessary and used specifically for the intended purpose.

Occasionally, we use our email lists to inform interested parties about new software releases, important product information, and promotional opportunities.

We may provide information collected in giveaways and promotions to nonaffiliated third parties who are our partners in those giveaways and promotions. We may provide information to those with whom we may jointly distribute our product. We may also hire other companies to provide us with such support as packaging, processing, and shipping. All receiving parties are required to keep the limited information provided in strict confidentiality and limit the use to the intended purpose for which it was disclosed.

We do not rent or sell mailing lists to any third party.

Security Standards

Information security is taken very seriously and we take precautions to prevent unauthorized access to your information by those who do not have a legitimate need for it. We will never post your email address without your prior consent.

Policy Changes

UltraBac Software may occasionally change or update this privacy policy. Please revisit this policy as often as you wish and refer to the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of the page.
