TechTarget's Storage Media Group Editors Recognize UltraBac Software

Bellevue, Wash. – (January 17, 2007) – UltraBac Software (www.ultrabac.com) today announced that UltraBac Disaster Recovery (UBDR) Gold v3.0 has been selected as a finalist for the 2006 Storage Products of the Year awards hosted by TechTarget's Storage Media Group.

After receiving almost two hundred nominations from vendors and IT pros alike, the editors at SearchStorage.com and Storage magazine are recognizing the finalists in each field. UBDR Gold v3.0 is being recognized in the Backup and Disaster Recovery Software category. Winners of the 2006 Storage Products of the Year awards will be announced in February.

"We are very proud to have our image disaster recovery product, UBDR Gold, recognized as a finalist in these awards," said Morgan Edwards, CEO of UltraBac Software. "It is a tribute to the features and the quality of our product for the editors of Storage magazine and SearchStorage.com to honor us with this distinction."

SearchStorage.com currently features editorial coverage of the finalists, including UBDR Gold v3.0, on its Web site.

About UltraBac Software
UltraBac Software has earned a reputation for providing reliable, fast, and innovative backup and recovery software to organizations of all sizes. Products include file-by-file and image based disaster recovery solutions. UltraBac is the company's flagship product, providing file-by-file backup capabilities with versatile backup options and flexible scheduling. UltraBac Disaster Recovery Pro and Gold (UBDR Pro and Gold) provide additional image based backup and disaster recovery capabilities which can literally restore a failed server to a bare metal drive in seconds. UltraBac Software solutions support backups and recoveries to dissimilar media of virtually any type including virtual machines. Optional agents for Exchange, SQL, Oracle, Tivoli, and NetWare extend the functionality of both solutions. Visit www.ultrabac.com for trial software, or for more information.

About Storage
