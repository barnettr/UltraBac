To help you increase your bottom line, we frequently offer additional short-time discounts or giveaways that are available only to our partners. These promotions are advertised using special emails, our partner newsletter, and the UltraBac Software Web site.

Internal Use License

Regular use of UltraBac Software products on your own equipment provides a good working knowledge of our solutions and will better prepare you to sell and support your customers. To encourage the internal use of our software, we supply not-for-resale licenses to our partners through two options. Option One offers a permanent license of the software with no expiration or restriction of use for 50% of list price. Option Two is a no-charge, twelve-month license that is subject to an annual performance review.
